# Garfield - Your Pet 🐱

A floating, interactive desktop companion that lives on your screen and cares about your health and productivity! 

Garfield is a frameless, transparent desktop pet designed to keep you company while you work, but more importantly, to ensure you are taking care of yourself during long computing sessions.

## Features & Capabilities

- **Desktop Companion:** A frameless pet that floats on your screen, keeping you company with custom animations (idle, swinging, and more).
- **Hydration Reminders:** Garfield will periodically notify you to drink water and stay hydrated while you work.
- **Screen-Time Alerts:** If you've been sitting and staring at the screen for too long, your pet will remind you to step away, go for a walk, and rest your eyes.
- **Exercise Scheduling:** Receive timely reminders to do stretches or exercise at specific times of the day to stay active.
- **System Tray Integration:** Discreetly sits in your system tray so you can access settings, open the app, or exit the pet companion without cluttering your taskbar.

## Getting Started

To run the Garfield Pet app on Windows:

```bash
flutter run -d windows -t lib/pet_main.dart
```

Take care of your virtual pet by letting it take care of you!
